The Cellar - Wine Bar in Paphos

Wine Collection

Experience one of the world's largest collections of Cypriot wines, featuring over 100 labels from all regions of Cyprus. Our selection includes both indigenous grape varieties, such as Xynisteri and Maratheftiko, and internationally renowned styles, carefully curated to showcase the rich diversity of the island's winemaking heritage.

Food Pairing

Complement your wine with our expertly crafted platters, featuring authentic Cypriot delicacies, aged meats, and a fine selection of cheeses. Enjoy warm pita bread with creamy hummus, enhancing every bite. Our pairings are designed to create a balanced and rich tasting experience, perfectly suited to our diverse wine collection.
